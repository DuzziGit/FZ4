Welcome to the second tutorial level.
In this level you will be introduced to the enemies that will be roaming the world.
The enemies you will be meeting are: Skeleton, Bat and Slime.
You will be able to practice your combat skills on them here.
This will get you ready for the big world and help you survive the beginer levels of the world.
Once you are finished training you can head towards the portal on the right side of the map to start your journey.
